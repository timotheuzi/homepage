Privacy Policy for Darknesses BBS

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Introduction

Darknesses BBS ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web-based terminal RPG.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you individually outside of the username you choose for your character.

2. Game Data

  • User Accounts: Character names, class, race, and progression stats.
  • Game Progress: Server-side storage of character inventories, locations, and achievements.
  • Usage Analytics: Basic connection logging to maintain server stability and detect multi-accounting.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the multi-user game experience
  • Maintain global leaderboards (TOP command)
  • Ensure fair play and prevent abuse

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on our secure servers.
  • We implement security measures to protect your account data.

Third-Party Services

  • No third-party analytics.
  • No data sharing with third parties.
  • No advertising.

Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.

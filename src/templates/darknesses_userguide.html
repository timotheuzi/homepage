Darknesses BBS — User Guide

Overview

Darknesses BBS is a multi-user, terminal-style cyberpunk RPG inspired by classic BBS games like MajorMUD. Players explore a procedurally generated grid, engage in tactical combat, customize their builds via manual stat training, and compete with other users in a gritty, neon-soaked world.

Getting Started

  1. Navigate to the terminal at darknesses.pythonanywhere.com.
  2. Click "Initialize New Profile" to create a character.
  3. Select your Augmentation Path (Race) and Operational Class.
  4. Log in and arrive at The Neon Hub, the grid's central safe zone.

Game Commands

Navigation & Session

  • N, S, E, W: Standard movement.
  • EXIT: Safely logs out.

Information

  • LOOK / L: Scans the sector for entities, players, items, and exits.
  • WHO: Lists all active users.
  • TOP: Displays the Top 10 adventurers.
  • ST / STATUS: Detailed profile view.
  • I / INVENTORY: Lists equipped and stored hardware.

Action & Progression

  • A / KILL <target>: Engage in combat.
  • TRAIN <stat>: Spend points on STR, INT, WIL, AGI, HEA, or CHA.
  • <ABILITY> <target>: Execute a class-specific ability.

World Structure

The grid contains 8 distinct sectors:

  • The Slums (Lv 1-3)
  • Neon District (Lv 2-5)
  • Industrial Zone (Lv 3-6)
  • Corporate Plaza (Lv 6-10)
  • The Under-Grid (Lv 8-12)
  • The Wastes (Lv 10-15)
  • Data Nexus (Lv 12-18)
  • The Undercity (Lv 15-20)

Combat System

Combat rounds are influenced by your equipped weapon's Speed Bonus. Your Attack scales with STR, and Defense scales with AGI.

PvP Protocol: Combat is allowed in all sectors except The Neon Hub. You can only attack players within 3 levels of your own.

Character Progression

Each level-up grants 5 Stat Points. Use the TRAIN command to improve:

  • STR: Increases Attack.
  • INT/WIL: Increases Max Mana and Ability Damage.
  • AGI: Increases Defense.
  • HEA: Increases Max HP.
  • CHA: Increases Trickster ability success.

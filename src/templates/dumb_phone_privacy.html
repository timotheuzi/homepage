Privacy Policy for Dumb Phone

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Introduction

Dumb Phone ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you individually.

2. App Usage Data

  • Network Logs: Local storage of firewall logs and blocklist data.
  • App Preferences: User settings for firewall modes and rules.
  • Usage Analytics: Basic usage patterns to improve app performance.

3. Permissions

The app requires permissions for:

  • VPN service to monitor network traffic
  • Storage for saving logs
  • No data is transmitted off-device

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide network security functionality
  • Maintain user preferences
  • Improve app performance

All data remains on your device.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No information is transmitted to external servers
  • We implement security measures to protect your data

Third-Party Services

  • No third-party analytics
  • No data sharing
  • No advertising

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.

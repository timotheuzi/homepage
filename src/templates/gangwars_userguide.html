Gangwar User Guide 🎮

Welcome to the streets. This guide will help you navigate the dangerous world of Gangwar and build your criminal empire.

🏁 Objective

The goal is simple: Survive and Dominate. Accumulate wealth, grow your gang, and eventually take down the legendary Squidie Army to prove you're the king of the streets.

📍 Key Locations

🏙️ City (Main Hub)

This is your primary navigation screen. From here, you can access all other locations in the city. Keep an eye on the top bar for your current status (Health, Cash, and Day).

🏚️ Crackhouse

The heart of your economy.

  • Buy/Sell: Trade in various substances. Prices fluctuate daily.
  • Strategy: Buy low in one area or day, and sell high when the market peaks.

🔫 Gun Shack

Your source for protection and firepower.

  • Arsenal: Buy handguns, submachine guns, and explosives.
  • Ammo: Don't forget to stock up; a gun without bullets is just a paperweight.
  • Armor: Essential for surviving late-game gang wars.

🏦 Bank

Safe-keep your hard-earned cash.

  • Deposit: Protect your money from being stolen if you lose a fight.
  • Loans: Need quick cash for a big deal? Take a loan, but watch the interest!

🍺 The Bar

The place for rumors and recruitment.

  • Recruit: Find new members to join your gang. Larger gangs deal more damage and can carry more loot.
  • Intel: Talk to the regulars to find out where the best deals or biggest threats are.

🏪 Pick n Save

General supplies and gang management.

  • Supplies: Buy items that help with travel or health.
  • Management: View your gang's detailed stats and equipment.

⚔️ Combat & Survival

Gang Wars

Randomly, or when provoked, you will enter combat with rival gangs.

  • Attack: Use your equipped weapons.
  • Defend: Your armor and gang size determine how much damage you can soak up.
  • Run: Sometimes survival is better than pride. Attempt to flee if the odds are against you.

Health & Death

  • If your health reaches 0, it's game over.
  • Visit the Infobooth or use supplies from Pick n Save to heal.

📈 Tips for Success

  1. Bank Your Cash: Never carry more money than you can afford to lose. If you're "wasted" on the streets, rivals will take your pocket cash.
  2. Grow Your Gang: More members mean more "mules" to carry drugs and more "soldiers" in a fight.
  3. Watch the Clock: You only have a limited number of days to make your mark. Make every move count.
  4. Upgrade Early: A basic pistol is better than nothing, but you'll want an Uzi or better before the first week is out.

🛠️ Controls

  • Tap: Select locations, buy items, or confirm actions.
  • Scroll: Navigate through long lists of inventory or drug types.
  • Back Button: Returns you to the City Hub from any location.

Good luck. You're going to need it. 💰🔫🏙️

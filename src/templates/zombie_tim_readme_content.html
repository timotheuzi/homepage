🧟 Zombie Tim - The Undead AI Assistant 🧟

Zombie Tim is the world's first undead AI assistant with a gory, beautiful UI, enhanced intelligence, and word-learning capabilities! Built with Flutter, Zombie Tim combines cutting-edge AI with zombie-themed humor, blood-splattered design, and powerful tech tools in one terrifyingly awesome package.

🚀 Latest Abilities

🧠 Enhanced Undead Intelligence

  • Dynamic Personality: Zombie Tim now has a deep obsession with BRAAAINS!
  • IQ Tracking: Tim's intelligence grows as he learns. Ask "what is your IQ?" to see his progress.
  • Contextual Learning: Tim now extracts both words and phrases from your conversations to better understand human speech patterns.
  • Improved RAG Service: Faster response times with intelligent caching and memory management.

📚 Massive Vocabulary Ingestion

  • Internet Feasts: Use the new "Mass Vocabulary Feast" in the Scan screen to fetch and devour 50 random words from the internet at once!
  • Bulk Import: Command Tim to import words: , to bulk-feed his undead brain.
  • Passive Learning: Tim continues to learn and evolve his vocabulary even when you aren't talking to him.

🔍 Gory Data Analysis

  • File Digging: Scan internal and external storage to find "delicious" data chunks.
  • Sniffing & Chewing: Analyze directories and individual files with high-performance scanning tools.
